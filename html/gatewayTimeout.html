What happened?

Think of it like waiting in line at a cafe, but the barista is taking a really long time to respond. That's basically what's happening when you see a 504 error. The website you're trying to visit is kind of like that barista, not responding in time because they are too busy or are having some issues.

What you can do?

Now, what can you do when you're stuck with a 504 error? Start with hitting the refresh button, or F5 on your keyboard. Just like how the line at the cafe might suddenly move faster, the website could start working too. However, if that doesn't change things, don't be disheartened. Maybe it's time for a quick break. You can return and try again after a few minutes. If you continue to come across this message often, you might want to notify the website owner about the problem. Generally though, such issues tend to be resolved pretty quickly. So stay calm and try again later!
